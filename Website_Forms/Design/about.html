Mission Statement


Hi everyone, I’m Shanna-kay. As someone riddled with anxiety and a chronic overthinker, candles have always provided me with the reprieve I seek. When I am overwhelmed with my thoughts and life, I create a safe space to filter out the unnecessary clutter in my mind and let myself breathe. Unbridled Luxe was birthed from the desire to have vegan, eco-friendly and non-toxic candles with a soothing crackling wick that relaxes you. Also, to make you escape to a place where you can unburden yourself with ease and without worries.


It was inspired by my quirky, flirty and playful personality, meant to emphasize the importance of being unapologetic. I have embraced the fact that I’ll never be everyone’s cup of tea and that is perfectly fine. And my hope is the same for everyone else as well.


Our handcrafted artisan candles aim to give you relief and be added to your self-care routine. Do what makes you unwind babe!
